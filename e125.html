What This Site Is About

“Ethics in Audio Engineering” is a resource hub for engineers, students, and community members who want to learn about responsible design, ethically sourced materials, data privacy, and how to engage communities in the engineering process. Our mission is to help you integrate ethical reasoning into every step of your work—turning abstract ideas into actionable best practices.
